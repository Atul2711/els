IN COMPANY OR CORPORATE LANGUAGE COURSES

EUROPE LANGUAGE SCHOOL offers high quality Language courses for companies located around the globe. If a company needs a Spanish / German/ French/ Portuguese Course with specific aims, adapted to the particular needs of its employees, Europe Language School will design a special program. Discounts are also offered on long term programs.

CULTURAL EVENTS

The EUROPE LANGUAGE SCHOOL offers a wide variety of cultural programmes with the objective of building bridges between Europe countries and India. The cultural programmes range from literature and thought to science and technology. These programmes are presented through arts, music, gastronomy, history, conferences, exhibitions, concerts, movie screenings, workshops, meetings between artists and researchers from Spain,Turkey,Germany , India and Latin America etc.The entry to the ACTIVITIES is generally free, unless mentioned. Though the programmes are all conducted in Spanish, they are always translated into English or any of the Indian languages thus allowing everyone to understand and appreciate the programmes.
