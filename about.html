About us

Who we are?

The Europe Language School is recognised across India and abroad for its worldwide network and cultural activities. We offer a range of specialized projects in arts, education, Europe languages and society to audiences across the globe. We provide access to (European languages) Spanish, German, French, Portuguese and English training and learning for both students and teachers and enable opportunities to study and work in Europe and abroad.

Our website offers all the language-related issues requested by students as well as by people connected with our language.

Hence, our objectives and purposes are as follows:

  • To organize general and special Foreign language courses, as well as courses.
  • To be up-to-date in our teaching methods and in the training of professors.
  • To support the work done by Europeans.
  • To participate in programs that aim to promote the European languages.
  • To carry out cultural promotion activities in collaboration with other organizations.
